IDKit is how you embed Proof of Human into your product. Proofs are zero-knowledge and unlinkable across apps, so your users can verify their World ID without revealing any information about themselves. To get started, you need to: install the SDK, register the app in the Developer Portal, generate a backend signature to prevent forgery, collect the proof from the user, and verify it server-side.

- [IDKit integration guide](https://docs.world.org/world-id/idkit/integrate)

MiniKit 2.0 enables you to deploy your app inside the World App and tap into our distribution. You can load docs.world.org/llms-full.txt into your AI coding tool for full MiniKit context.

- [MiniKit 2.0 quick start docs](https://docs.world.org/mini-apps/quick-start/installing)

AgentKit lets you register an agent wallet against a verified World ID, then use that signal on your side to grant preferred access or offer discounts to human-backed agents. It accepts payments on both World Chain and Base using x402.

- [AgentKit integration docs](https://docs.world.org/agents/agent-kit/integrate)
