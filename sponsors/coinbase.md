Coinbase Developer Platform gives Frontier teams a practical stack for agentic commerce: x402 for paid APIs, CDP facilitator infrastructure, Agentic Market service discovery, onramps/offramps, and wallet products for users, backends, and autonomous agents.

x402 lets builders charge for APIs, data, tools, and content over HTTP with instant stablecoin payments. The CDP facilitator handles payment verification and settlement, while the x402 MCP server shows how agents can call paid APIs without manual checkout.

- [x402 Docs](https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/x402/welcome)
- [x402 MCP Server](https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/x402/mcp-server)
- [Agentic Market](https://agentic.market/)

For product flows that need funding or custody, Coinbase Onramp & Offramp support fiat-to-crypto funding and crypto-to-fiat cash out. The wallet guide helps teams choose between embedded wallets, server wallets, and agentic wallets; the Agentic Wallet Skills repo gives agents prebuilt actions for funding, sending USDC, trading, finding x402 services, and paying for them.

- [Onramp & Offramp](https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/onramp/introduction/welcome)
- [Wallet Product Guide](https://docs.cdp.coinbase.com/server-wallets/comparing-our-wallets)
- [Agentic Wallet Skills](https://github.com/coinbase/agentic-wallet-skills)
