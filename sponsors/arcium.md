Arcium brings encrypted computation to blockchains, making it possible to build applications where data remains private by default. This opens up a new design space beyond transparent smart contracts, enabling private DeFi, encrypted AI, and entirely new market primitives.

Using Arcis, Arcium's Rust framework, developers can quickly build and deploy encrypted applications. In the hackathon, we encourage teams to explore this design space, whether that's dark pools, private perps and lending, blind auctions, encrypted voting, raffles and lotteries, or entirely new market primitives such as opportunity markets. Arcium also enables primitives like threshold signing and secure randomness, along with capabilities such as access control.
