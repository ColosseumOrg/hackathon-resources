Swig is building the future of smart wallets for agents, consumers, and enterprises. Our open-source SDKs and APIs enable deep programmability with a robust onchain policy engine, gasless payments, delegated execution, and zk identity. Swig was designed for developers with a low CU, trust-minimized architecture, and is used by leading agentic finance apps, crypto neobanks, consumer DeFi apps, and institutions.

What to build with Swig: Programmable smart wallets are well suited for several use cases: agentic payments, automated DeFi strategies, web2-style consumer apps, real-world payments, corporate treasury, or institutional finance.
