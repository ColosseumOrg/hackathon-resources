Vanish is a privacy layer for Solana that lets traders execute without exposing their strategy on-chain and without compromising compliance. It breaks the link between a public wallet and on-chain activity while preserving full control over routing and execution, and integrates directly into existing swap flows with no impact on performance or UX.

- [Developer Docs](https://core.vanish.trade)
- [API Reference](https://core.vanish.trade/api-reference)

Vanish is offering $10,000 to the team that builds the best integration using the Vanish Core API. Build private swaps in places they haven't existed before — Telegram trading bots that swap without linking wallet history, portfolio rebalancing tools that rotate positions without revealing strategy, AI agents that execute trades without exposing the agent's wallet, or payment flows where merchants accept tokens privately. Private trading with Vanish adds ~200ms to trade finality, works with any DEX aggregator, and requires no changes to your existing RPC setup.
