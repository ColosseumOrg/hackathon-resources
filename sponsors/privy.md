Privy provides embedded wallet infrastructure that lets you onboard users and build full onchain experiences without managing wallets yourself. With a single integration, you can create self-custodial wallets for every user and enable seamless interaction with Solana apps. This goes from funding and swaps to DeFi participation, all within your product.

- [Docs](https://docs.privy.io)
- [Agent CLI + Sandbox](https://agents.privy.io)

For the Colosseum hackathon, we're especially excited to see teams go beyond basic wallet creation and showcase end-to-end onchain flows. Use Privy to power experiences like in-app swaps, earning on idle balances, or gasless transactions that abstract away friction for users. Whether you're building a consumer app, trading product, or fintech experience on Solana, Privy gives you the primitives to make it feel fast, seamless, and invisible.
